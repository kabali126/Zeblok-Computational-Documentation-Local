## The Platform at a Glance

Zeblok's **Ai-MicroCloud™** is a turnkey, cloud-native AI environment that eliminates 12–18 months of engineering effort to integrate the technologies required for a production AI ecosystem. It covers the full ML lifecycle — from development and training to testing and deployment — spanning Core to Edge.

---

## Workspace

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Ai-WorkStation" icon="book-open">
    A single unified JupyterLab environment that orchestrates infrastructure, open-source AI/ML frameworks, and proven AI algorithms. Write code, run experiments, visualise results, and take models to production — all on one platform.

    - **Default resources:** 1 GPU · 1 vCPU · 64 GB storage
    - Scales easily as your workload grows
    - Accessible to data scientists, data engineers, and AI engineers
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ai-HPC-WorkStation" icon="microchip">
    Extends the standard Ai-WorkStation for large-scale, compute-intensive workloads. Ideal for training large models that require multi-GPU parallelism or long batch jobs.

    - **4+ GPUs** for demanding training runs
    - **Slurm-as-a-Service** for job scheduling and workload management
    - **Parallel processing** across worker nodes
    - Same JupyterLab interface — no workflow change required
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ai-MicroServices" icon="cube">
    Deploy containerized services on demand from the platform. Choose from public templates such as nginx, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, MLflow, and ChromaDB — or import your own custom image. Configure ports, environment variables, replicas, and compute plans to match your workload.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ai-Inference" icon="rocket">
    Deploy large language models as live, queryable endpoints. Interact with your model through a built-in chat interface or query it programmatically via an OpenAI-compatible API. Supports autoscaling based on GPU utilisation thresholds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ai-API™ Engine" icon="bolt">
    Bridges the gap between data science and DevOps. Deploy trained ML models anywhere as production REST APIs — fast, repeatable, and scalable — without requiring deep infrastructure knowledge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Agents" icon="robot">
    Build and deploy autonomous AI agents that reason, plan, and act. Connect agents to tools, APIs, and data sources to automate complex multi-step workflows.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Gen AI Workspace

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Knowledge Distillations" icon="brain">
    Compress large foundation models into smaller, faster variants that retain accuracy. Distilled models are cheaper to serve and faster to respond — ideal for production deployments with latency or cost constraints.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Domain Adaptations" icon="sliders">
    Fine-tune foundation models on your own domain-specific data to improve task performance. Adapt general-purpose models to specialised use cases such as legal, medical, or financial language.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Platform

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AI Model Hub" icon="rocket">
    A central registry of AI models available on the platform. Import models directly from Hugging Face — public or private — and make them available for inference deployment. The hub tracks model status, version, serving image, and endpoint details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ai-Data Lake" icon="database">
    A centralised, high-performance data store for structured and unstructured data at any scale.

    - Operates at **SSD speeds** for fast read/write access
    - **Industry-grade SSL security** with data redundancy
    - Scales up as your data grows
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Ai-MicroCloud™ Manager" icon="shield-halved">
    The centralised administration utility for the entire platform. Organisation administrators can manage every component from one place.

    - **RBAC** — role-based access control for users and teams
    - **Plans** — configure vCPUs, GPUs, memory, and storage
    - **Namespaces** — isolate workloads across teams and projects
    - **Metering** — view resource utilisation for billing or chargeback
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## JupyterLab

The interface you work in when you launch an Ai-WorkStation or Ai-HPC-WorkStation. JupyterLab is a web-based interactive development environment for notebooks, code, and data.

It is flexible, extensible, and modular — you can arrange the interface to support a wide range of workflows in data science, scientific computing, and machine learning, and extend it with plugins that integrate with existing tools.

<Tip>
  New to JupyterLab? The [official JupyterLab documentation](https://jupyterlab.readthedocs.io) is a great place to get started.
</Tip>
