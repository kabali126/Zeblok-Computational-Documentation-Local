## Overview

The Ai-MicroCloud platform supports four account types — **Individual**, **Enterprise**, **Playground**, and **Partner** — each designed for a different use case. Registration is a 3-step process: choose your account path, fill in your details, and verify your email.

To get started, navigate to your Ai-MicroCloud platform URL and click **Create one** at the bottom of the sign-in panel.

## Step 1 — Choose Your Account Type

On the registration page, select the account type that matches your use case. Four options are available as tabs at the top of the form:

| Account Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **Individual** | For personal use or invited workspace access |
| **Enterprise** | For teams onboarding with organisation details |
| **Playground** | For trial access with a lighter setup |
| **Partner** | For partner onboarding with compliance and business details |

---

## Step 2 — Fill in Your Account Details

Once you select an account type, the form updates to show the fields relevant to that path. Fill in all required fields marked with `*` and agree to the Terms and Conditions, then click **Continue**.

    - **Email** — your email address
    - **Username** — a unique handle, minimum 3 characters

    Check **I agree to the Terms and Conditions** and click **Continue**.

  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Enterprise">

## Step 3 — Verify Your Email

After clicking **Continue**, the platform sends a **6-digit verification code** to the email address you registered with. This is Step 3 of 3 in the onboarding flow.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/signup-and-login/enter-code.png" alt="Email verification screen showing Step 3 of 3 with six input boxes for the 6-digit code, a Verify Email button, and a Resend option" />
</Frame>

- Enter the 6-digit code in the boxes provided
- Click **Verify Email** to complete registration
- If you did not receive the code, click **Resend** after the countdown timer expires
- To go back and correct your details, click **Back to Register**

<Note>
  Check your spam or junk folder if the verification email does not arrive within a few minutes. The code expires after a short period — use the Resend option if needed.
</Note>

## Sign In

Once your account is verified, you can sign in at any time from the login page.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/signup-and-login/login-using-creds.png" alt="Sign In form with Email or Username and Password fields filled in, with Sign In button and Sign in with Google option visible" />
</Frame>

- Enter your **Email or Username** and **Password**
- Optionally check **Remember me** to stay signed in
- Click **Sign In**
- Alternatively, click **Sign in with Google** to authenticate using your Google account

<Tip>
  If you have forgotten your password, click **Forgot password?** on the login page to reset it via email.
</Tip>

---

## You're In

After a successful sign-in, you will be redirected to the **Platform Management** dashboard — your central hub for managing infrastructure, users, and configurations across the Ai-MicroCloud platform.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/signup-and-login/logged-in.png" alt="Platform Management dashboard showing Identity and Access, Data Centers, Plans and Resources, and Configurations cards, along with Infrastructure stats, Resource Overview, and Platform Summary" />
</Frame>

From here you can access your **Workstations**, **Microservices**, **Inference** endpoints, **Agents**, and all platform configuration tools from the left sidebar.
