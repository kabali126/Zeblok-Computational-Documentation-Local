---
title: "Introduction"
description: "Zeblok Computational is a GPU-powered AI/ML platform that gives you a unified environment to develop, train, and deploy machine learning models at scale."
---

Zeblok Computational provides a single, pre-configured cloud environment where data scientists, data engineers, and AI engineers can access datasets, GPU-powered AI/ML frameworks, languages, tools, and AI algorithms — all from one place.

At the core of the platform is the **Ai-WorkStation**, built on JupyterLab, which lets you write and run code, mix in equations and visualisations, and collaborate with your team without setting up any infrastructure. For high-performance workloads, the **Ai-HPC-WorkStation** adds Slurm-as-a-Service and parallel processing across four or more GPUs.

The entire platform runs inside **Ai-MicroCloud™** — a turnkey, cloud-native AI environment that spans from Core to Edge and covers the full ML lifecycle: development, testing, training, and production deployment.
